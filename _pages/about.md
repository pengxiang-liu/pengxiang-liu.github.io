I am a postdoctoral researcher at the [School of Data Science](https://www.sdsc.cityu.edu.hk), [City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk), working with [Prof. Zijun Zhang](http://www.cityu.edu.hk/stfprofile/00308048.htm).

Previously, I received the B.S. degree from [Zhengzhou University](https://www.zzu.edu.cn), Zhengzhou, China, in 2017; and the M.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees from [Southeast University](https://www.seu.edu.cn), Nanjing, China, in 2019 and 2023, all in Electrical Engineering. From 2022 to 2023, I was sponsored by China Scholarship Council (CSC) as a visiting Ph.D. student to study in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk), UK, supervised by [Prof. Goran Strbac](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/g.strbac).

My research focuses on **machine learning (ML)** and **operation research (OR)** with applications in modeling, design and optimization of urban morphology and multi-energy systems.
